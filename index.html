Product Name: FitQuest

Team Members: Jose Montes De Oca Morfin

Application Overview: This application is designed for people who are new to the gym or their fitness journey, and dont know exactly what workouts to do, or just need a little guidance on some exercises.

  • Node.js
  • Express.js
  • React.js
  • TailwindCSS
  • Email.js
  • Passport.js
  • MongoDB

Proposal Updated: November 10th, 2023

  • Ability to see a list of all exercises for a specific body part.
  • Ability to send an email with a question about any fitness topic.
  • Recive any email information to a database to save them.
  • Authentication for different users.
  • Ability to save their favorite excerices to a favorties tab.
